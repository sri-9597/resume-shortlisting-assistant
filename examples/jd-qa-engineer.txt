QA Engineer — Sample Job Description
=====================================

We're hiring a QA engineer who will own automated testing across our product.
This is an automation-first role: if you prefer to test only manually, this
isn't the right fit.

What you'll do
--------------
- Design and grow our automation suites: API tests, end-to-end browser tests,
  contract tests where appropriate.
- Decide what to automate vs. what to leave manual — your judgement matters more
  than your line count.
- Integrate tests into CI/CD; own flaky-test mitigation, parallelization,
  and test infrastructure.
- Partner with engineers on bug triage, root-cause analysis, and writing
  high-signal bug reports.
- Champion testability concerns during feature design.

What we're looking for
----------------------
- 3+ years of professional QA experience.
- Hands-on experience writing automated tests in at least one framework
  (Playwright, Cypress, Selenium, Pytest, RestAssured, etc.).
- Programming skill — your automation code should be reviewable, not just runnable.
- Strong written communication: your bug reports save engineers time.
- Bonus: experience with performance testing, accessibility testing, or
  test-data-management strategies.

Working arrangement
-------------------
Fully remote. Async-first.
