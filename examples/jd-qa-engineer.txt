QA Engineer — Job Description
=====================================
About the Role:
-------------------

We're looking for a QA Engineer who takes quality personally. You'll work closely with our engineering team to ensure our products are reliable, well-tested, and release-ready. This is a hands-on role that spans both automation and manual testing, and you'll have real ownership over quality processes, not just be a gatekeeper at the end of a pipeline.

What You'll Do
-------------------

• Design, build, and maintain automated testing frameworks from the ground up and iterate on them as the product evolves
• Write, review, and improve automated test suites covering unit, integration, and end-to-end testing
• Manually test new features thoroughly before releases, including exploratory testing and edge case coverage
• Own the release sign-off process — be the last line of defense before something ships
• Integrate automated tests into CI/CD pipelines and ensure test runs are fast, reliable, and actionable
• Identify flaky tests, debug failures, and keep the test suite healthy over time
• Work with engineers to reproduce bugs, define acceptance criteria, and validate fixes
• Maintain clear, up-to-date documentation — test plans, test cases, release checklists, and bug reports
• Contribute to improving QA processes and standards across the team

What We're Looking For
-------------------

• 2 to 4 years of experience in a QA or SDET role with a mix of automation and manual testing
• Hands-on experience building or significantly contributing to automated testing frameworks (Playwright, Cypress, Jest, or similar)
• Solid understanding of CI/CD pipelines and experience integrating tests into them (GitHub Actions or similar)
• Experience testing modern web applications, including REST APIs and frontend interfaces
• Strong debugging skills and the ability to triage failures independently
• Comfort with JavaScript or TypeScript — our stack is primarily TypeScript/Node.js
• A methodical, documentation-first mindset
• Good communication skills — you'll be coordinating sign-offs and surfacing quality issues clearly Nice to Have
• Experience testing Next.js applications or SSR/SSG-based frontends
• Familiarity with cloud environments (AWS) and database-level testing (MongoDB)
• Exposure to performance or accessibility testing
• Experience working on multi-tenant or data-heavy application

Working arrangement
-------------------
Fully remote. Async-first.
