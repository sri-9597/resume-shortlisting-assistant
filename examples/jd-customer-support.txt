Customer Support Specialist — Sample Job Description
=====================================================

We're hiring a customer support specialist to own day-to-day customer
communication for our SaaS product. Most of the job is written: tickets,
help docs, internal triage notes.

What you'll do
--------------
- Triage and respond to inbound customer support tickets (mostly written,
  occasional video calls).
- Investigate technical issues end-to-end — read logs, check the DB, escalate
  to engineering only when you've narrowed the cause.
- Write and maintain help-center articles. Identify high-volume topics and
  pre-empt tickets with documentation.
- Feed customer pain back into product: weekly themes, escalation patterns,
  feature-request synthesis.
- Train new teammates on tools and workflows as the team grows.

What we're looking for
----------------------
- 2+ years of customer-facing support experience, ideally in SaaS or B2B tech.
- Excellent written English — clear, concise, warm.
- Technical aptitude: comfort with APIs, log files, browser dev tools.
- Customer empathy demonstrated by past escalations, CSAT/NPS improvements, or
  documentation work.
- Bonus: experience with mainstream support tooling (Zendesk, Intercom, HubSpot,
  Front) and an interest in support ops / scaling support.

Working arrangement
-------------------
Fully remote. Coverage window expected: ~9am–5pm in EU/UK timezones, with
on-call rotation for after-hours escalations.
