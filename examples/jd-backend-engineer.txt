Backend Engineer — Sample Job Description
==========================================

We're hiring a backend engineer to own services that power our product. You'll
work across the stack but spend most of your time in the API/data layer.

What you'll do
--------------
- Design and ship backend services in our primary stack (Node.js + TypeScript or
  Java + Spring — we'll match to your strengths).
- Own features end-to-end: design doc → implementation → deploy → on-call.
- Make data-model decisions: schema design, indexing, query optimization,
  migrations under load.
- Review PRs, write technical docs, mentor more junior engineers.
- Participate in our weekly rotation to handle production incidents.

What we're looking for
----------------------
- 4+ years of professional backend experience.
- Production experience with at least one mainstream backend stack and one
  database technology (relational or document).
- Comfortable with the full delivery cycle — not just writing code, but
  shipping, observing, and iterating on what you've shipped.
- Clear written communication; comfortable working asynchronously across
  timezones.
- Bonus: experience with our specific stack (Node/TypeScript, Java/Spring,
  MongoDB, AWS, Python), or with high-throughput data pipelines.

Working arrangement
-------------------
Fully remote. Async-first. ~4 hours/day of overlap with EU/UK timezones expected.
